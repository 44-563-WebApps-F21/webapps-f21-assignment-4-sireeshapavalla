Vera or, The Nihilists

BARON RAF


1.BARON RAFF. If they had you would have missed a charming dance.

2.BARON RAFF. A cook and a diplomatist! an excellent parallel. If I had ason who was a fool, I'd make him one or the other.

3.BARON RAFF. Yes.[5] What a mistake it is to be sincere!

4.BARON RAFF. One has only one head, you know, Prince.

5.BARON RAFF. Mes amis, I am extremely sorry for you. It is out of the question.

6.BARON RAFF. I am sure of it; at least he told me he had sent two telegrams already to Paris about his dinner.

7.BARON RAFF. He is exiled.

8.BARON RAFF. The devil!

9.BARON RAFF. Sire, you are adding another horror to exile.
